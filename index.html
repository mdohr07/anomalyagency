Our Services

We investigate unexplained phenomena, using our combined knowledge of science and law enforcement. We also specialize in forensic analysis and criminal psychology, and we approach each case with a methodical and open mind, always seeking the truth.

Our approach is open-minded, methodical and thorough. We approach each case with a clean slate and follow the evidence wherever it leads. Our investigative methods are based on a solid combination of science and law enforcement, utilizing technology while also respecting the importance of intuition and the human factor. We also consider how the case is related to other events, how the events are connected and whether there is a larger pattern at play. We always seek the truth, using facts and critical thinking to analyze evidence.

  • Research and analysis of unexplained phenomena

    Examining events and phenomena that cannot be explained by science or conventional wisdom. Utilizing technology available to analyze evidence and seek out clues.

  • Psychological profiling and profiling of the environment

    Examining the patterns, thoughts and feelings of criminal suspects and witnesses. Also, analyzing the environment of the crime scene to find clues and understand the situation of the victim and suspect.

  • Development of case strategies and planning

    Mapping out a plan to pursue evidence, find connections and solve the case.

Furthermore...


  • Crime scene investigation and forensic analysis
  • Information gathering and interviews with witnesses, suspects, etc.
  • Consultations with law enforcement personnel and other government agencies
  • Research and analysis of historical cases in order to locate connections between cases
